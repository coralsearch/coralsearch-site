Our Story

We Are the Most Trustworthy
Search Partners for Decades.

Coral Search is an executive search firm operating in a few specialised industry verticals. We are the most trustworthy search partners to our clients and have associations of decades with most of our clients.

We have an expertise in Talent Acquisition across all industry verticals including IT (Digital Product Management, Data Sciences/Analytics, RPA, Cloud Architecture/Development, Infrastructure, Program Management, SEO, UX/UI etc.), Finance, Strategy & Transformation, Sales & Marketing, Supply Chain, HR, R&D, Market Research, and Packaging.

Our team comes with superior background and have experience working with hiring managers, HRBPs, and global stakeholders to determine their requirements and supporting them in final decisions on candidate hiring; making recommendations on appropriate hires and being advisors to the stakeholders.
