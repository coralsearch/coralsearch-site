Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the search process take? +
Timelines vary by role complexity. Typically, our executive search process takes 3-6 weeks from briefing to shortlist. Retained searches for C-suite roles may take 6-10 weeks to ensure the perfect fit.
What industries do you specialize in? +
We specialize across all major industry verticals including IT & Digital, Finance & Strategy, Sales & Marketing, Supply Chain, Human Resources, and R&D. Our team has deep domain expertise in each area.
What makes CoralSearch different? +
We build decade-long relationships with our clients. Unlike transactional recruiters, we become strategic partners — understanding your culture, goals, and talent needs at a deeper level. Our 98% client retention rate speaks for itself.
Do you offer a placement guarantee? +
Yes. For retained search engagements, we offer a 6-month replacement guarantee. For permanent recruitment, we offer a 3-month guarantee. We stand behind every placement we make.
How do you screen candidates? +
Our screening process includes initial competency assessment, in-depth interviews, formal reference checking, and where required, psychometric evaluation. We present only candidates who meet all your stated requirements.
Do you work with international organizations? +
Yes. CoralSearch works with global organizations placing talent across geographies. Our network spans multiple countries and we have experience managing cross-border executive searches.
